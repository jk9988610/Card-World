Card World

Everything is a card. Click a card to Play it.

Start here

  1. On the Field (table), click World Controller.
  2. Programming cards appear in your Hand. A Door appears on the Field.
  3. Click the Door — its text becomes Open.
  4. Drag cards between Hand and Field. Click any card to Play it.

You are the first Binder. These programming cards are how you weave the world.
